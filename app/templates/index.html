Why blood donation matters

A small act can become someone's second chance.

Donated blood supports patients during emergencies, surgery, cancer treatment and complications during childbirth. Because blood cannot be manufactured, a dependable supply begins with voluntary donors.

01

Support critical care

Blood donations help hospitals respond to trauma, major surgery and urgent medical treatment.

02

Help more than one need

After processing, different blood components may be used to support different patients.

03

Strengthen local readiness

A verified donor community gives requesters a better starting point when time is limited.

Final donation eligibility and blood-group confirmation are always decided by an authorized blood bank.
